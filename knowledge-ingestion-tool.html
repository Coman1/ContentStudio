Drawdown guidance — income limits

Owner: Pensions Operations · v1.4 · May 2025

Overview

Drawdown allows customers to take flexible income directly from their pension fund while remaining invested. Income limits depend on whether the customer is in flexi-access drawdown or capped drawdown (pre-April 2015 arrangements only).

Flexi-access drawdown

There is no maximum income limit under flexi-access drawdown. Customers may withdraw any amount at any time, subject to their fund value.

Capped drawdown — 2025/26 GAD rate update

⚠️ Edit in progress: GAD rate updated 2.75% → 3.25% effective 6 April 2025. Maximum income = 150% of GAD annuity rate × fund value.

Understanding capped drawdown
Pensions ops guide · v1.3 · Live
LivePensionsRestricted

What is capped drawdown?

Capped drawdown is available to customers who entered drawdown before 6 April 2015. The maximum income is limited to 150% of the GAD annuity rate.

Switching from capped to flexi-access drawdown triggers the MPAA. Always confirm arrangement type before advising.

Income limits

GAD reference rate for 2025/26 is 3.25%. Maximum income = 150% of GAD annuity rate × fund value. Reviews required at each pension birthday or within 3 years.
