Understanding capped drawdown
Pensions ops guide · v1.3 · Live
LivePensionsRestricted

What is capped drawdown?

Capped drawdown is available to customers who entered drawdown before 6 April 2015. The maximum income is limited to 150% of the GAD annuity rate.

Switching from capped to flexi-access drawdown triggers the MPAA. Always confirm arrangement type before advising.

Income limits 2025/26

GAD reference rate: 3.25%. Maximum income = 150% × GAD annuity rate × fund value. Reviews at each pension birthday or within 3 years.
